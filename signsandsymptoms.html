What is Depression?

Depression is a popular mental illness among young people. It will negatively affect your feeling, your behaviours, and how you think. Depression may cause mood down or make you lose your interest in any activity that you once enjoyed. It can lead to emotional and physical problems and can affect the close relationship with your work partners and families. Fortunately, depression can be relieved and treatable. With proper diagnosis and treatment, the vast majority of people with depression will overcome it. If you are experiencing symptoms of depression, a first step is to see your family physician or psychiatrist. Talk about your concerns and request a thorough evaluation. This is a start to addressing your mental health needs.
