Depression can happen to anyone

60% of International students studying in Victoria are dealing with Depression and other mental health conditions

Call: Lifeline 13 11 14

More than 200,000 international students from different countries choose to study in Victoria. It was reported in the 2016 Census that there were a total of 201,548 persons in the 15 to 24 age group who were attending university students in Victoria.

As stated in the Australian Bureau of Statistics, 4.8 million or 20% of Australians had a mental or behavioural condition. In Australia, 1 in 2 adults will go through some type of mental health condition at a point in their lives.
